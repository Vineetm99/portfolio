About

I am a final-year Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering student at Pune Institute of Computer Technology (PICT). My journey began with a childhood curiosity about how wireless devices communicate, which has matured into a disciplined pursuit of becoming an architect of technology. I am deeply fascinated by the complex architectures that keep our society connected, specifically in the realms of VLSI and intelligent networks.

My academic path is defined by a transition from theoretical understanding to practical application. Whether it's debugging simulations for UAV networks or mentoring underprivileged students in STEM, I am driven by a desire to bridge the gap between hardware and software to solve real-world problems.
